Our Story

We Are Aarvi IT Solutions

Founded in 2017, Aarvi IT Solutions began with a simple mission: to be the technology partner that small and medium businesses deserve — reliable, responsive, and result-oriented.

What started as a small software consultancy has grown into a full-service IT company delivering custom software, long-term maintenance support, and comprehensive IT services to over 50 clients across India and abroad.

Our team of seasoned developers, architects, and support engineers brings deep technical expertise combined with genuine care for our clients' success. We believe technology should enable business growth — not create headaches.
